This project was made during my internship at Procore Technologies. I worked on it with three other interns to create a full-stack application for the recruiting department. We created everything ourselves with some ideas from what the recruiting team wanted. I worked on mainly the frontend for this project.

Without revealing too much, this project was made to replace the way that interviewees received confirmation interview emails. Instead of an email, they will receive a link to their personalized dashboard containing all of other necessary information that they would need for their interview. These things included a breakdown of their schedule, what they should bring, how to get there, and etc.
